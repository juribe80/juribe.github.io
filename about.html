    The Americano is an American restaurant nestled in historic downtown Hightstown, New Jersey. Established in 2003, The Americano serves delicious burgers, sandwiches, and other classics.We now have 4 locations, all serving the same menus. We do offer seasonal options that may now appear on our web-based menu.

    We are conveniently located in the Town Square of each location. Come on in for a good time and yummy meal. We're open at each of our four locations:


    • Monday-Thursday & Sunday from 11am-10pm
    • Friday & Saturday from 10am-12am
